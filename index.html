Gensyn Protocol

Connecting the world's GPUs into one unified network for AI training

The Vision

Make AI development accessible to everyone by creating a global marketplace for compute power. Anyone with a GPU can contribute. Anyone building AI can access affordable compute. No gatekeepers. No monopolies.

AI training costs are skyrocketing - models like GPT-4 cost $100M+ to train

Billions of dollars worth of GPU capacity sits idle worldwide

Centralized compute creates barriers to entry for AI innovation
